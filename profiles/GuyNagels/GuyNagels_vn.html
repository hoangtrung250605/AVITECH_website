• Trưởng nhóm nghiên cứu AIMS

As associate chair of neurology in the UZ Brussel, my clinical focus is diagnosis, treatment and rehabilitation in multiple sclerosis (MS). Cognition is an important though often underrated aspect of MS, and this has caused my research focus to grow into the modelling of cognition in MS and other neurodegenerative disorders.
